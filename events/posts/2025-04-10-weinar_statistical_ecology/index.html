Youn-ISA Webinar Joint with the BIR Committee on Statistical Ecology

Date: Thursday April 10th, 2025
Location: Online
Time: 12 pm - 2:30 pm GMT
Theme of the event: Statistical Ecology

The Young Statisticians’ Section of the Irish Statistical Association (Young-ISA) was formed in 2019 to create and cultivate a network of career-young statisticians across Ireland, supporting their professional development and providing a link to the Irish Statistical Association.

We are thrilled to announce our joint webinar with the British and Irish Region (BIR). The Young-ISA webinar joint with the BIR Committee on Statistical Ecology aims to bring together many career-young statisticians in Ireland to focus on the application and theoretical development of statistical ecology.

Invited Speakers

Prof Caroline Brophy, Professor in Statistics, Trinity College Dublin, Ireland.

Dr Virginia Morera-Pujol , Postdoctoral Research Fellow, University College Dublin, Ireland.

Dr Rafael de Andrade Moral, Associate Professor in Statistics, Maynooth University, Ireland.

Rebecca Akeresola, PhD Student in Statistics, University of Edinburgh, UK.
